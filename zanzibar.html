Paradise Found in the Indian Ocean

Discover Zanzibar

Zanzibar, the Spice Island, is a tropical jewel off Tanzania’s coast, renowned for its turquoise waters, white-sand beaches, and rich history. From the labyrinthine alleys of Stone Town—a UNESCO World Heritage Site—to the fragrant spice plantations and vibrant coral reefs, Zanzibar offers a perfect blend of relaxation and exploration. With Tanzania Trek, your custom escape unveils the island’s magic, from dhow cruises to cultural immersion.

Travel Tips for Zanzibar

Make the most of your island escape with these insider tips from Tanzania Trek.

  • Best Time to Visit: June to October for dry, sunny days; December to February for warm weather.
  • What to Pack: Swimsuit, sunscreen, light clothing, and a hat—beach vibes all the way.
  • Cultural Respect: Dress modestly in Stone Town and ask before photographing locals.
  • Stay Hydrated: Sip coconut water or local juices to beat the tropical heat.
