Serengeti Express

3-Day Tanzania Safari Itinerary

Serengeti Safari Itinerary Overview

The Serengeti Express is a thrilling 3-day *Serengeti safari itinerary* plunging you into Tanzania’s iconic plains. Chase the Great Migration, spot the Big Five, and camp under starlit skies with Tanzania Trek’s expert Maasai guides. This *Tanzania safari tour* blends raw adventure and authentic vibes—perfect for wildlife lovers craving the ultimate *Great Migration tour*. See all tours.

Serengeti Express Daily Schedule

Day 1: Arrival & First Safari

Land in Arusha, transfer to Serengeti National Park, and kick off your *Serengeti safari itinerary* with an afternoon game drive. Spot lions, zebras, and giraffes before settling into your eco-camp for a fireside dinner.

Day 2: Great Migration Chase

Rise early for a full-day *Great Migration tour*—track millions of wildebeest and zebras thundering across the plains. Picnic lunch in the bush, sunset drive, and overnight at camp with tales from your Maasai guide.

Day 3: Morning Safari & Departure

Start with a sunrise game drive—catch elephants and cheetahs in action. After breakfast, transfer back to Arusha, ending your *Tanzania safari tour* with memories of Serengeti’s wild heart.
