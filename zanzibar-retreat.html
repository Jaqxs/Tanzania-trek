4-Day Tanzania Beach Tour

Zanzibar Safari Itinerary Overview

The Zanzibar Retreat is your 4-day *Tanzania beach tour*, diving into turquoise waters, spice-soaked alleys, and coastal chill with Tanzania Trek. Snorkel Mnemba’s reefs, taste cloves in Stone Town, and unwind on powder sands—perfect for adventurers craving a *Zanzibar safari itinerary* with soul. Swap bush dust for ocean salt on this *Zanzibar retreat vacation*. See all tours.

Zanzibar Retreat Daily Schedule

Day 1: Arrival & Stone Town Vibes

Touch down in Zanzibar, hit Stone Town’s maze—haggle fish, sip spiced kahawa under starlight. Check into your beachfront digs to kick off your *Tanzania beach tour*.

Day 2: Snorkeling Mnemba Atoll

Dive into the *Zanzibar safari itinerary*—boat to Mnemba Atoll, snorkel coral reefs alive with parrotfish and rays. Beach lunch, then chill on Nungwi’s sands.

Day 3: Spice Tour & Sunset Dhow

Roam a spice farm—cloves, vanilla, cinnamon bite the air. Afternoon’s yours on the beach, then catch a sunset dhow cruise—pure *Zanzibar retreat vacation* magic.

Day 4: Beach Bliss & Departure

Last soak in turquoise waves, breakfast with ocean whispers, then roll out to the airport—your *Tanzania beach tour* ends with salt-crusted memories.
