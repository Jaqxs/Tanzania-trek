A Tapestry of Mountains and Wildlife

Discover Arusha National Park

Arusha National Park, a 137-square-kilometer sanctuary near Arusha city, is a mosaic of lush forests, rolling hills, and shimmering lakes, all dwarfed by the majestic Mount Meru. Often overshadowed by its famous neighbors, this park offers intimate wildlife encounters and stunning scenery. With Tanzania Trek, your custom tour explores this hidden gem, from giraffe-filled plains to the serene Momella Lakes.

Travel Tips for Arusha

Make the most of your Arusha adventure with these tips from Tanzania Trek.

  • Best Time to Visit: June to October for dry weather and clear Meru views.
  • What to Pack: Binoculars, layered clothing, and sturdy shoes for walking safaris.
  • Explore on Foot: Take a guided walking safari for a closer connection to nature.
  • Day Trip Friendly: Arusha’s proximity to the city makes it perfect for short visits.
