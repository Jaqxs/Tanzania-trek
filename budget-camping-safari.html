Budget Camping Safari

6 Days of Wild Adventure on a Budget

About This Budget Camping Safari

Experience Tanzania’s iconic wildlife on a 6-day budget camping safari through Tarangire, Serengeti, and Ngorongoro Crater. Sleep under the stars in cozy tents, enjoy guided game drives, and immerse yourself in nature—all at an affordable price with Tanzania Trek.

Your Day-by-Day Camping Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Arusha

Arrive at Kilimanjaro International Airport (JRO) and transfer to Outpost Lodge in Arusha. Meet your guide, enjoy a budget-friendly dinner, and rest up for the adventure.

Day 2: Tarangire National Park

Drive 2.5 hours to Tarangire, known for elephant herds and baobab trees. Full-day game drive, then camp at Tarangire Public Campsite with basic amenities.

Day 3: Serengeti National Park

Head 3.5 hours to Serengeti’s vast plains. Afternoon game drive to spot the Big Five. Set up camp at Seronera Public Campsite under the starry sky.

Day 4: Serengeti Exploration

Full-day game drive in Serengeti—track wildlife and the Great Migration (seasonal). Optional balloon ride ($550). Another night at Seronera Campsite.

Day 5: Ngorongoro Crater

Drive 3 hours to Ngorongoro. Descend into the crater for a 6-hour game drive—see black rhinos and more. Camp at Simba Public Campsite with crater views.

Day 6: Departure

Return to Arusha (4 hours) with an optional market stop. Transfer to JRO for your flight, carrying budget safari memories.

Cost Breakdown for Your Budget Safari

Total Price: $1,500–$1,800 per person (double occupancy)

  • Includes: Camping gear, meals, park fees, English-speaking guide, 4x4 safari vehicle, transfers
  • Excludes: International flights, visa ($50-$100), tips, optional balloon safari, travel insurance
