Honeymoon Safari & Zanzibar

10 Days of Romance and Adventure

About This Honeymoon Adventure

Celebrate your love with a luxurious 10-day journey—safari in Serengeti’s wild plains and a romantic beach escape in Zanzibar. Tanzania Trek curates an unforgettable honeymoon with private touches and stunning landscapes.

Your Honeymoon Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Arusha

Land at Kilimanjaro International Airport (JRO). Check into Arusha Coffee Lodge with a welcome champagne toast.

Day 2: Serengeti National Park

Fly to Serengeti (1 hour). Afternoon game drive with a private sundowner. Stay at Four Seasons Safari Lodge.

Day 3: Serengeti Romance

Full-day safari with a gourmet picnic lunch. Optional hot air balloon ride ($550). Second night at Four Seasons.

Day 4: Serengeti Exploration

Another day of wildlife—lions, cheetahs, and more. Private dinner under the stars. Third night at Four Seasons.

Day 5: Ngorongoro Crater

Drive 3 hours to Ngorongoro. Crater game drive with a romantic picnic. Stay at The Manor at Ngorongoro.

Day 6: Zanzibar Arrival

Fly to Zanzibar (2 hours via Arusha). Check into Zuri Zanzibar with a couples’ welcome massage.

Day 7: Zanzibar Beach Bliss

Relax on white sands or swim in turquoise waters. Private beach dinner included. Second night at Zuri.

Day 8: Spice Tour & Stone Town

Morning spice plantation tour, afternoon in Stone Town. Third night at Zuri Zanzibar.

Day 9: Zanzibar Leisure

Free day—snorkel, sail, or unwind. Farewell rooftop cocktails. Fourth night at Zuri.

Day 10: Departure

Fly back to Dar es Salaam (1 hour) or JRO (via Arusha). Depart with honeymoon memories.

Cost Breakdown for Honeymoon

Total Price: $5,500–$6,000 per person (double occupancy)

  • Includes: Lodging, meals, park fees, guide, 4x4 vehicle, domestic flights, transfers, private experiences
  • Excludes: International flights, visa ($50-$100), tips, balloon ride, insurance
