Discover Tarangire

Tarangire National Park, spanning 2,850 square kilometers, is a hidden gem in Tanzania’s northern circuit. Famous for its massive elephant herds, ancient baobab trees, and the life-giving Tarangire River, this park offers a quieter, yet equally thrilling safari experience. With Tanzania Trek, your custom journey reveals Tarangire’s untamed landscapes and abundant wildlife, from lions in the treetops to birds in the sky.

Travel Tips for Tarangire

Maximize your Tarangire safari with these insider tips from Tanzania Trek.

  • Best Time to Visit: June to October for the dry season, when animals gather at the river.
  • What to Pack: Binoculars, sunscreen, and a hat—perfect for open savannah exploration.
  • Safari Timing: Early mornings or late afternoons for cooler weather and active wildlife.
  • Stay Alert: Elephants can be close—follow your guide’s advice for safety.
