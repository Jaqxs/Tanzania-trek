Discover the Serengeti

The Serengeti National Park is Tanzania’s beating heart—a vast expanse of 14,750 square kilometers where nature reigns supreme. Famous for the Great Migration, where over 1.5 million wildebeest and hundreds of thousands of zebras and gazelles traverse the plains, this UNESCO World Heritage Site offers unparalleled wildlife spectacles. With Tanzania Trek, your custom safari unveils the Serengeti’s secrets—from predator-prey dramas to serene sunsets over acacia-studded horizons.

Travel Tips for the Serengeti

Maximize your safari adventure with these insider tips from Tanzania Trek.

  • Best Time to Visit: June to October for the dry season and migration; December to March for lush landscapes and fewer crowds.
  • What to Pack: Binoculars, lightweight layers, sunscreen, and a good camera—wildlife waits for no one!
  • Safari Etiquette: Keep a respectful distance from animals and follow your guide’s lead for safety.
  • Hydration is Key: The sun can be intense—bring a refillable water bottle.
