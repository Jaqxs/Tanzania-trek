Where Lions Climb and Flamingos Flock

Discover Lake Manyara

Lake Manyara National Park, a compact 330-square-kilometer gem, sits at the base of the Great Rift Valley escarpment. Known for its tree-climbing lions, vibrant flamingo flocks, and lush groundwater forest, this park offers a diverse safari experience in a stunning setting. With Tanzania Trek, your custom adventure uncovers the serene beauty and wild surprises of Lake Manyara.

Travel Tips for Lake Manyara

Enhance your safari with these expert tips from Tanzania Trek.

  • Best Time to Visit: July to October for dry weather and flamingo sightings.
  • What to Pack: Binoculars, insect repellent, and a camera for the forest and lake.
  • Safari Timing: Morning drives for cooler air and active animals.
  • Stay Observant: Tree-climbing lions can be elusive—trust your guide’s keen eyes.
