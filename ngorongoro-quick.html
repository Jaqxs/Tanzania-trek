Ngorongoro Quick

2-Day Crater Safari

Ngorongoro Quick

Dive into the Ngorongoro Crater, a UNESCO World Heritage Site teeming with wildlife, on this fast-paced 2-day safari—perfect for spotting the Big Five with expert guides.

Daily Schedule

  • Day 1: Crater Descent & Game Drive - Arrive at Ngorongoro,
  • Day 2: Spice Tour - Explore spice plantations, taste cloves and cinnamon, relax on the beach.
  • Day 3: Stone Town & Snorkeling - Morning tour of Stone Town’s markets and history, afternoon snorkeling in coral reefs.
  • Day 4: Leisure & Departure - Free morning for swimming or shopping, transfer to airport.

Accommodation Options

  • Comfort: Beachfront resort with private rooms and ocean views.
  • Budget: Guesthouse near the beach with shared facilities.

Inclusions

  • Local guides for tours
  • All meals (breakfast, lunch, dinner)
  • Spice tour and snorkeling gear
  • Accommodation as per selected tier
  • Transfers to/from Zanzibar airport

Pricing: $900 per person (Comfort) / $700 per person (Budget) - Based on a minimum of 2 travelers
