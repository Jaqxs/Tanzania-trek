Luxury Serengeti & Crater Fly-In Safari

6 Days of Premium Wilderness Bliss

About This Adventure

Elevate your safari with this 6-day luxury fly-in experience, soaring over Tanzania’s Serengeti and Ngorongoro Crater. Stay in premium lodges, enjoy private game drives, and cap it with a hot-air balloon ride. Perfect for those seeking exclusivity and comfort in the wild.

Your Day-by-Day Journey

Day 1: Arrival in Arusha

Land at Kilimanjaro International Airport (JRO) and settle into the Four Points by Sheraton Arusha. Enjoy a gourmet dinner and a briefing for your luxury adventure.

Day 2: Fly to Serengeti

Take a 1-hour private flight to Serengeti. Afternoon game drive in a luxury 4x4. Check into Four Seasons Safari Lodge Serengeti with infinity pool views.

Day 3: Serengeti Exploration

Full-day private game drive tracking the Big Five and Great Migration (seasonal). Evening sundowners and a second night at Four Seasons.

Day 4: Serengeti Balloon Safari

Rise early for a hot-air balloon ride ($550, included) over the Serengeti, followed by a champagne bush breakfast. Relax at Four Seasons for your final night.

Day 5: Ngorongoro Crater

Fly 45 minutes to Manyara airstrip, then drive 1 hour to Ngorongoro. Descend for a private crater tour—spot black rhinos. Stay at Ngorongoro Crater Lodge.

Day 6: Departure

Morning at leisure with crater views. Fly back to Arusha (1 hour), then transfer to JRO for your flight home, wrapped in luxury memories.

Cost Breakdown

Total Price: $4,500–$5,500 per person (double occupancy)

  • Includes: Domestic flights, premium lodging, all meals, balloon safari, private guide, 4x4 vehicle, park fees, transfers
  • Excludes: International flights, visa ($50-$100), tips, travel insurance
